# Subnetting II

## Relation between network, broadcast address and subnet mask
The network address of the next subnet mask is equal to one plus the broadcast address of the current network's subnet mask.

### Subnetting Trick
![](images/Pasted%20image%2020260318003331.png)

Find the last weight from the network portion and keep adding that to get successive network addresses of all subnets.
